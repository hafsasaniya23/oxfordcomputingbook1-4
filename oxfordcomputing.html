Book 2

Grade 2–3 · Ages 7–8

Building confidence — moves into operating system tasks, word processing, and early digital citizenship.

Computer Hardware Operating System Word Processing (MS Word) Formatting Text Inserting Pictures Saving & Opening Files Printing Basics Introduction to Email Digital Safety Basics Searching the Web
Difficulty Elementary

Book 3

Grade 3–4 · Ages 8–10

Expanding skills — introduces spreadsheets, presentations, and deeper understanding of technology.

Advanced Word Processing Tables in Documents Spreadsheets (MS Excel) Charts & Graphs Simple Formulas Presentations (MS PowerPoint) Slides & Transitions Internet & Networks Cyber Safety Computational Thinking
Difficulty Intermediate

Book 4

Grade 4–5 · Ages 9–11

Advanced level — mastering productivity tools, web concepts, and preparing for text-based coding.

Advanced Word (Mail Merge) Advanced Spreadsheets Functions (SUM, AVG, etc.) Advanced Presentations Web Browsing Skills Introduction to HTML Introduction to Databases Cloud Computing Basics Digital Ethics & Privacy How Computers Work
Difficulty Advanced
